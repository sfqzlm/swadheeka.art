About

I’m a passionate and versatile ui/ux designer. crafting intuitive and engaging user journey

I’m a self taught designer, based in germany with two years of experience. specialising in ui/ux design with a strong focus on enhancing user experience. i enjoy creating thoughtful and unique user interfaces for mobile and web.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of being part of several creative teams. I interned at Rig Group, a Kolkata-based company in India, as a UI/UX designer, where I gained valuable real-world knowledge in areas like design systems, user research, and aligning design with business goals. Before that, I worked as a graphic designer at Tridz in Bangalore, India, for one and a half years. I hold a degree in multimedia. Beyond my work as a designer, I am also passionate about drawing, painting, and other creative pursuits."
