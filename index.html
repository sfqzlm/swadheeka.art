About

I’m a passionate and versatile ui/ux designer, crafting intuitive and engaging user journeys

I’m a self taught designer based in germany with two years of experience, specialising in ui/ux design with a strong focus on enhancing user experience. i enjoy creating thoughtful and unique user interfaces for both mobile and web.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the privilege of being part of several creative teams. I interned as a UI/UX designer at Rig Group, a Kolkata-based company in India, where I gained valuable real-world experience in design systems, user research, and aligning design with business goals. Prior to that, I worked as a graphic designer at Tridz in Bangalore, India, for one and a half years. I hold a degree in multimedia. Beyond my work as a designer, I am also passionate about drawing, painting, and other creative pursuits."
